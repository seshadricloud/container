Detect Issue (error rate spike, latency increase)\n2. Trigger Rollback Command\n3. Kubernetes reverts to previous image\n4. Health checks verify old version\n5. Traffic redirected to stable version\n6. Incident investigation begins\n7. Fix prepared and redeployed\n```\n\n---\n\n## рҹ‘Ҙ Team Structure\n\n### Team Composition (40-50 people)\n\n#### 1. **Infrastructure Team (8-10 people)**\n```\nLead: Infrastructure Manager\nв”ңв”Җ
